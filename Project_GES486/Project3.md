<b>UMBC Campus map based on the buildings and point locations<b>
  
**Project description:**
  
<b>This map was created using ArcMap software. I created this map for people who are on the UMBC campus and can how many buildings we have and where certian points of hotspots are located. Each buildings are represented by a polygon that was drawn out. The map signifies how many buildings are on campus and are the most clustered on the campus. The points represent the hotspot areas on campus, so the UC Ballroom building is the most popular.<b>
